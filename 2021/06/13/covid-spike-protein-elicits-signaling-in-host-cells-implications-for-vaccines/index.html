Thought experiment:

Imagine this spike protein, either from COVID or a vaccine causes a chain of events causing ones children to be infertile.

There are various environmental things that can cause offspring to be infertile, so the scenario isn't totally unreasonable.

We probably wouldn't figure that out for ~20 years.

By that point, probably 95% of earth's population will have been vaccinated or exposed.

For a newborn to be fertile, they need two non-affected parents. So the chances of that might be 0.05*0.05 = 0.0025.

What would society look like with a generation where 99.75% of people are infertile?

Luckily the chances of my hypothetical happening are nearly nil... But it's still scary!
