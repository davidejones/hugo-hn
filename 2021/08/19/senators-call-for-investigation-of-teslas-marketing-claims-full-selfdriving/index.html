Senators Call for Investigation of Tesla’s Marketing Claims, 'Full Self-Driving'

rvz 2021-08-19 05:05:29 +0000 UTC [ - ]

From [0]

> Tesla’s marketing has repeatedly overstated the capabilities of its vehicles, and these statements increasingly pose a threat to motorists and other users of the road. Accordingly, we urge you to open an investigation into potentially deceptive and unfair practices in Tesla’s advertising and marketing of its driving automation systems and take appropriate enforcement action to ensure the safety of all drivers on the road.

So even several US Senators believe that Tesla's deceptive advertising of the FSD capabilities is worth investigating and poses a threat to other drivers on the road. The same argument I have made weeks ago [1] and [2].

I don't think there is any defense in this sort of advertising, as the term 'Full Self Driving' is obviously misleading and the system is still incomplete, still in beta and is unreliable itself.
