0 A.D. A free, open-source game of ancient warfare

awb 2021-08-17 02:03:52 +0000 UTC [ - ]

For those new to 0 A.D., it's been in development for years and just released a new version (Alpha 25) last week.

Alpha is an understatement IMO as this game has great cross-platform support, a multi-player lobby, pretty balanced gameplay and amazing graphics, plus a ton of other features too numerous to mention.

There's also a huge scripting component in JavaScript, so it's pretty easy for most devs to contribute maps and make gameplay PRs.

There was also a long delay between v23 and v24, but now the team seems back on track and committed to a 6 month release cycle.

To play: https://play0ad.com/

To contribute: https://trac.wildfiregames.com/
